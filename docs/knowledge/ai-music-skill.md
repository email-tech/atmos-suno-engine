# AI Music Creation Skill Specification

## Overview

This skill defines how the app approaches AI-assisted music creation: using AI tools for lyrics, melodies, arrangements, stems, and full tracks, while keeping human musical judgment and ethics at the center.

## Core Subskills

- Tool understanding: knowing what different AI music tools can do (lyrics, composition, stems, vocals).
- Prompt design: writing clear, structured prompts that specify style, mood, instrumentation, structure, and reference points.
- Iteration and curation: generating multiple outputs, evaluating them, and selecting or combining the best material.
- Hybrid workflow design: integrating AI-generated content with human performance, arrangement, production, and mixing.
- Ethics and copyright awareness: understanding usage rights, originality, and responsible deployment.

## Key Concepts

- AI tools generate patterns based on training data; they are starting points, not replacements for musical judgment.
- Strong prompts specify musical and emotional intent: genre, tempo, instrumentation, lyrical themes, and structural cues.
- Curation is critical: most AI outputs will be average; the skill lies in spotting and developing the few strong ideas.

## Tools, Techniques, and Workflows

- Prompting workflow: define goal, constraints, and references; write prompt; generate; review; refine.
- Hybrid workflow: import AI stems into the DAW, add human parts, edit and mix into a cohesive track.
- Quality control: check musical coherence, technical quality, and fit with the users artistic identity.

## Learning Progression

### Beginner

- Use AI to generate simple ideas (lyrics, loops) and explore styles.

### Intermediate

- Design multi-part prompts, build structured songs, and incorporate AI content into serious projects.

### Advanced

- Build consistent AI-assisted workflows for specific genres and personal styles, balancing efficiency with artistic control.

## Common Mistakes and Misconceptions

- Treating AI outputs as finished products rather than drafts.
- Writing vague prompts that yield generic results.
- Ignoring ethical and legal considerations.

## Cross-Links to Other Skills

- Musical Foundations: informs structured prompts and evaluation of AI-generated harmony, melody, and rhythm.
- Songwriting & Composition: guides refinement of AI-generated lyrics and melodic ideas.
- Production & Mixing: turns AI ideas into professionally produced tracks.
