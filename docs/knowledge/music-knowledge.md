# Music Knowledgebase Overview

This file describes the disciplines and documents that make up the music knowledgebase for the app.

## Disciplines and Core Documents

- Musical Foundations (Music Theory & Ear Training)
  - `docs/music-theory-skill.md`
- Songwriting & Composition
  - `docs/songwriting-skill.md`
- Arrangement & Instrumentation
  - `docs/arrange-skill.md`
- Production, Recording, Editing & Mixing
  - `docs/prod-mix-skill.md`
- Hardware, Monitoring & Studio Setup
  - `docs/hardware-studio.md`
- AI Music Creation
  - `docs/ai-music-skill.md`

## How Claude Should Use This Knowledgebase

1. Identify which discipline(s) a user request or app feature involves.
2. Open the corresponding `*-skill.md` files and follow their definitions, subskills, workflows, and common mistakes.
3. When combining disciplines (e.g. theory + songwriting + mixing), follow the cross-links sections in the skill files to keep the app coherent.
4. Treat external textbooks, open educational resources, and manufacturer guides as backing references; the skill files summarize and normalize their content for app use.
